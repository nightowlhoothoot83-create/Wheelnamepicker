Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

🔒 Short version: We don't collect personal data. The tool runs in your browser. We use Google AdSense to display ads, which may use cookies. That's it.

This Privacy Policy describes how Wheel Name Picker (wheelnamepicker.com.au), operated by Ascension Digital Group, handles information when you use our website. We are committed to your privacy and to compliance with Australian privacy law.

1. Who We Are

Wheel Name Picker is operated by Ascension Digital Group, an Australian digital content and tools business. Our other properties include MyCalcTools.net and MyCalendarTools.net.

Contact: ascensiondigitalagency@outlook.com

2. Information We Collect

Information You Enter Into the Tool

Any names or items you add to the spinner wheel are stored only in your browser's local storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers. It is cleared when you close your browser tab. We cannot see, access or collect the content of your wheel lists.

Images You Upload to the Picture Wheel

If you use the 📷 image upload option to attach a photo to a wheel item, that image is converted to a temporary local data format and held only in your browser's memory for the current session. Images are never uploaded to our servers and are never transmitted anywhere — the picture wheel works entirely on your device. Closing or refreshing the tab clears any uploaded images.

Coin Flip, Dice Roller & Lucky Dip

These mini tools generate results using your browser's random number function. No input data, results or history from these tools is collected, stored or transmitted to us in any way.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically collect standard server log data when you visit, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymised where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Date and time of access

This data is used for security, performance monitoring and aggregate traffic analysis only. It is not linked to any personal identity.

3. Cookies

We use cookies in the following ways:

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

Analytics Cookies

We may use basic analytics to understand how many people visit the site and which features are used most. This data is aggregate and anonymous.

Essential Cookies

Our site may use minimal session or preference cookies required for the tool to function correctly (such as storing your sound preference). These do not track you across sites.

For more detail on how cookies are used on this site, see our Cookie Policy. Our Terms of Use set out the conditions for using the tools on this site.

4. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We participate in Google AdSense, which is operated by Google LLC. As a third-party vendor, Google uses cookies to serve ads on our site. These cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing behaviour.

Google's privacy policy is available at: policies.google.com/privacy

Our Google AdSense publisher ID is: ca-pub-1904958390525375

We do not control the content of third-party advertisements. Advertisers do not have access to any personal information collected by this website.

5. Children's Privacy

While Wheel Name Picker is commonly used in educational settings including primary and secondary schools, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The tool does not require any registration or personal data submission. Names entered into the spinner are stored only in the local browser session and are never transmitted or stored on our servers.

Parents and teachers should be aware that Google AdSense advertising may appear on this site. Teachers using this tool in classrooms may wish to check their school's policy on advertising-supported websites.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data at risk of breach from our systems. Any information you enter into the wheel spinner stays entirely within your browser.

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), you have the right to:

As we do not collect or store personal information beyond server logs, there is generally no personal information held about individual users. If you have a privacy concern, contact us at ascensiondigitalagency@outlook.com.
