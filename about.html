About Wheel Name Picker

Wheel Name Picker is a free online random spinner designed for Australian teachers, students, families and anyone who needs a fair, transparent and fun way to make a decision or run a competition.

Whether you're a classroom teacher picking the next student to answer a question, a parent deciding what's for dinner, or someone running a social media giveaway — Wheel Name Picker gives you an instant, equal-chance result with no sign-up, no downloads and no fuss.

What It Does

The Wheel Name Picker lets you:

  • Add any list of names, options, numbers or items to the wheel
  • Spin for a randomly selected result, weighted equally
  • Remove picked items so each person or option only comes up once
  • Track your pick history within the session
  • Use preset modes for common use cases — including Names, Numbers, Colours, Days of the Week, Chores, Dinner, Venue, Games and more
  • Create a fully custom wheel with your own items — including a picture wheel using uploaded photos
  • Flip a free online coin for heads or tails
  • Roll one or two free online dice
  • Generate free lucky dip / lotto-style random number sets

Who It's For

🏫 Teachers & Classrooms

Australian teachers use the Wheel Name Picker as a fair, visible random student selector. Add your class roll and spin to pick who answers next, assigns tasks, earns rewards or presents their work. Removing each student after they're picked ensures everyone gets a turn before anyone goes twice — no favouritism, no arguments.

🏆 Competitions & Giveaways

Need a fair winner for a competition, raffle or prize draw? Add all your entrants, spin live, and everyone can see the transparent result. The pick history records each draw so you have a clear record. Perfect for social media competitions, school fundraisers, sports club draws and workplace giveaways.

🎮 Games & Activities

Use the wheel to decide who goes first in board games, assign teams, pick game types, generate forfeits or run party game activities. The Games mode comes pre-loaded with popular activity options.

👨‍👩‍👧 Families

End household debates — from what's for dinner to whose turn it is to choose the movie. The chore wheel, day picker and dinner decider modes are built for family life.

How It's Funded

Wheel Name Picker is completely free to use and always will be. The site is supported by Google AdSense advertising. Ads allow us to keep the tool running, cover hosting costs and continue building new free tools for the community. We do not collect personal data, require accounts or charge for any features.

Technical Notes

The spinner runs entirely in your browser using HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. No data is sent to any server. Your custom lists are stored temporarily in your browser's local storage and are cleared when you close the tab. The random selection uses JavaScript's Math.random() function, giving every item an equal statistical probability of being selected on each spin.
