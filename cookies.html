Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🍪 Short version: We use a small number of cookies — mostly from Google AdSense for advertising. We don't use cookies to identify you personally.

This Cookie Policy explains how wheelnamepicker.com.au ("the Site"), operated by Ascension Digital Group, uses cookies and similar technologies. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember preferences, and provide information to site owners and advertisers.

2. Cookies We Use

TypePurposeSet By
AdvertisingServes and personalises ads, measures ad performanceGoogle AdSense
PreferencesRemembers your sound on/off setting while on the pageThis site (local storage)
AnalyticsAggregate, anonymous traffic and usage statisticsThis site / hosting provider

3. Google AdSense Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertising on this Site. Google and its advertising partners use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites, in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may interest you.

You can learn more about how Google uses cookies for advertising at policies.google.com/technologies/ads, and manage your ad personalisation settings at google.com/settings/ads.

4. Local Storage (Not a Cookie, But Similar)

Your wheel item lists, sound preference, and any uploaded picture-wheel images are stored using your browser's local storage — not as a cookie, and not transmitted to our servers. This data stays on your device and clears when you close the tab or clear your browser data.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers let you control cookies through their settings. You can usually:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from all sites
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Note that blocking all cookies may affect ad personalisation but will not stop the wheel, coin, dice or lucky dip tools from working — those run entirely on local storage and JavaScript, not cookies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for legal reasons. Check back periodically for updates.
